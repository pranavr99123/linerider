Shortcuts

`1-6` tools, `Ctrl/Cmd+Z` undo, `Shift+Ctrl/Cmd+Z` redo, `Space` play pause, `R` reset, `F` follow, `G` grid, `D` debug.

Quick Walkthrough

Draw tracks with `Freehand`, `Line`, or `Curve`. Use `Pan` to click-drag around the world and the mouse wheel to zoom.

Pick a line type to change how the rider behaves. `Boost`, `Brake`, `Ice`, `Bounce`, and the rest all affect physics differently.

Switch to `Play` when you want to simulate. `Pause`, `Reset`, and `Step` are for inspection and debugging.

`Start`, `Checkpoint`, `Finish`, `Powerup`, and world-object tools place gameplay pieces directly into the track.

`Ctrl/Cmd+Z` undoes edits, `Select` moves a segment, and `Eraser` removes nearly anything under the cursor.
